Glossary

Some IDEs (such as WebStorm) may require you to download the support library to use the Glossary. Simply click "Download" in the Glossary board and wait for the download to complete.

Daily Word

The Daily Word displays random words daily from the glossary, one by one. You can also enable it by using the One Word a Day action (no default shortcut).

You can also display these words in the IDE at startup by checking the "Show daily word at startup" option on the Settings page.In addition, Daily Word hides word definitions by default. To disable hiding definitions, check the "Display word definitions by default" option on the Settings page.
